Hello there!

My name is Elin Fernhede and I’m a game developer based in Sweden. I have studied Game Writing between 2022-2025 at the University of Skövde, a place full of creative game developers.

In my free time, I love playing D&D and write out my characters' backstories, reading fantasy books and playing games with my friends!

As a writer, I love story-driven games that tackle deep topics such as mental health, morality and the consequences of choice. Stories that make the player think and feel.

For more information, contact me or scroll down to see some examples of my work!

My Skills

I believe that video game stories are told using all the mediums in a game. It is a collaborative effort between writing, design, music, art and programming that comes together to tell immersive stories unique to video games. Because of this I care about communication, teamwork and adaptability in my work.

My strengths as a game writer lies mainly in worldbulding, characters and dialogue. Ever since childhood I've had a love for vast, immersive worlds that suck me in, both in books and video games. I love using the world as a tool to tell stories, using the setting to support the story in a way only it can. Furthermore, I care about creating characters that feel real, who talk and act like real people and whose stories can touch the players' hearts.

Portfolio

Here is a selection of stories and games I have previously worked on that showcase my writing style and abilities:

Kindled Blood

Kindled Blood is an exploration-focused Action-Adventure game I developed with a group of other students for a game design course at the University of Skövde.

"Anpassa sig bör man?"

An interactive Twine game about moral relativism. Created for a course on moral philosophy in game narratives at the University in Skövde. My teachers later used this game as an example at a conference when talking about said course.

A short story about a teenager searching for their mother in the midst of a war. Originally written in Swedish.
